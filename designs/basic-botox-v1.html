What You Will Learn

Our comprehensive program bridges the gap between clinical theory and aesthetic artistry through six core modules.

Facial Anatomy

Deep dive into musculature, nerve pathways, and vascular danger zones to ensure patient safety and precision.

Neurotoxin Pharmacology

Understanding onset, duration, diffusion patterns, and dilution protocols for optimal product performance.

Upper Face Techniques

Mastering the frontalis, glabella, and crow's feet with advanced placement strategies for natural results.

Lip Sculpting

Techniques for natural volume, border definition, and correcting asymmetry using various dermal filler types.

Mid-Face Rejuvenation

Contouring and structural support for the malar area to restore youthful volume and lift.

Complication Management

Critical safety protocols, adverse reaction identification, and emergency response management (Hyaluronidase).

The Clinical Edge
